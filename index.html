INFORMATION

  • tools:
    • select: to move or scale the page
      you can move the page from any tool by clicking the middle mouse button and move
    • pencil: to write on the page
    • brush: to draw on the page, you can change the size by scroll the mouse wheel or from the size box.
    • eraser: to erase from the page.
    • rectangle: to draw a rectangle you can change fill color and stroke color and size, you can click shift while drawing to draw a square.
    • line: to a line, you can click shift while drawing to draw a perfect line.
    • ellipse: to draw an ellipse, you can click shift while drawing to draw a circle.
  • click controle+z to go back and controle+y to go forward.
